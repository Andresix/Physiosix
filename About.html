Inspiration behind
Top notch Physio

  • Inaccessibility of proper health care to an average-income man
  • Peoples' poor awareness on health matters and healthy life style
  • Increasing morbidity and mortality rate associated to some
    common health conditions such as stroke, myocardial infarction, diabetes, etc

Goals of Top notch Physio

  • To increase the availability of good healthcare to everryone
  • To increase the level of awareness on health-related issues
  • To improve the quality of life of everyone, including people
    with any form of impairment
  • To reduce the morbidity and mortality rate associated with preventable
    and manageable disease conditions
  • In summary contributing tothe maintenance of a healthy life style
    by the general population
