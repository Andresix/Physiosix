Importance of exercise

Definition:

Exercise is a systematic, planned bodily movements, posture or physical activities
done to treat impairment or to maintain a healthy body.

Principles guiding exercise:

  • Frequency:
    This is referring to the number of exercise sessions one does.
    For example one could have their exercise session 4 times a week.
    For an effective exercise plan, it is advised to have a 30 minute-session plan,
    5 days per weeek.
  • Intensity:
    Intensity has to do with how hard you work during exercise.
    How you can change the intensity depends on the type of workout you're doing.
    For example running requires more energy than jogging, that is running is of a
    higher intensity than jogging.
  • Type:
    Type of exercise is simply kind of exercise being performed.
    There are different types of exercise such as aerobics, strengthening programmes,
    flexibility exercises, etc.
    The type of exercise prescribed is dependant on the required goal of the client/patient and clinician.
  • Time:
    The next element of your workout plan is how long you exercise during each session.
    There isn't one set rule for how long you should exercise,
    and it will typically depend on your fitness level and the type of workout you're doing.
    For example 30-60 minutes session of aerobic exercises for 3-5 days per week could be
    recommended depending on the person's level of fitness.

Health benefits of exercise

  • Exercise helps in weight reduction. Regular exercise burns the excess fat stored in the body.
  • Regular exercise reduces the chances of coming down with cancers such as colorectal cancer, breast,
    breast cancer,etc.
  • It reduces the chances of coming down with heart diseases by making our heart stronger and improving
    our circulatory functions
  • It reduces the chances of having type ii diabetes by increasing the sensitivity of cells to insulin
    and thereby facilitating the movement of sugar in the blood into the appropriate cells.
  • It makes one's bone stronger and reduces the chances of having pathological fractures
  • It helps control depression and improves one's mental health

There are so many other health benefits of exercise.
Therefore, regular exercise is required for a healthy life.
