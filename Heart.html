Heart attack

Introduction:

Heart attack which is also called Myocardial infarction is the death of a part of
the heart muscle as a result of reduced or lack of blood supply to that part.

Symptoms of heart attack

  • The major symptom is tightness and chest pain which could radiate to the neck and upper arm
  • Dizziness
  • Shortness of breath
  • Clammy and sweaty feeling
  • Nausea

Risk factors

  • Hypertension
  • High level of bad cholesterol
  • Diabetes
  • Family history of heart attack
  • Obesity
  • Smoking
  • Previous heart attack
  • Atherosclerosis/Arteriosclerosis

There are so many other risk factors associated to heart attack

Treatment of heart attack

  • Beta blockers
  • Angiotensin converting enzyme (ACE) inhibitor
  • Aspirin and other antiplatelets
  • Angioplasty
  • Coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG)

Prevention

The best way to prevent the occurrence of heart attack is by living a healthy life.
Some of the best ways to live a healthy life include the following:

  • Avoid smoking
  • Regular exercise
  • Check your blood pressure regularly to ensure it is within the normal range
  • Monitor your cholesterol level to ensure it is within the safe range
  • Reduce the intake of alcohol
  • Get adequate sleep
  • Maintain a healthy weight

Heart attack is one of the leading cause of disability and death.
So please let's live a healthy life and report to the hospital if one feels any of the
related syptoms to heart attack.
