Obesity

Introduction:

Obesity is a complex disease involving an excessive amount of body fat. Obesity isn't just a cosmetic concern.
It is a medical problem that increases your risk of other diseases and health problems,
such as heart disease, diabetes, high blood pressure and certain cancers. Being obese is influenced by both genetic and
environmental factors. By environmental factors I mean your choice of diet and lifestyle generally.

How to determine obesity

In most cases obesity can be determined by Body Mass Index(BMI) which is the division of body weight (W) in Kg by
the square of height in meter (H^2).

BMI (Kg/m^2) Weight status
18.5 Underweight
18.5-24.9 Normal
25-29.9 Overweight
30 and above Obesity

BMI is not used all the time to determine obesity, because in some very muscular athletes their BMI fall under excess range
not because of excess fat but rather due to muscle mass. Another method used is the waist-to-hip ratio.
It is calculated by dividing the circumference of the waist measurement by that of the hip measurement.
This method can also be used to assess the health risk due to excess fat.

Waist-to-hip ratio

Health risk Women Men
low 0.80 or lower 0.95 or lower
Moderate 0.81-0.85 0.96-1.0
High 0.86 or higher 1.0 or higher

Causes of Obesity

  • Consumption of excess carbohydrate and fatty foods
  • Adoption of a sedentary life style
  • late night eating
  • irregular exercise
  • Inadequate sleep
  • Alcohol consumption
  • Consumption of some drugs causes gain in weight e.g some steroids and antidepressants

Complications of obesity

  • Heart diseases
  • Stroke
  • Type 2 diabetes
  • Different types of cancers
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Sleep apnea

There are so many other diseases one can be predisposed to because of obesity

Prevention and control of obesity

  • Regular exercise
  • Consumption of healthy diet. Ensure adequate consumption fruits and vegetables
  • Get enough sleep (6-8 hours daily)
  • Take enough water
  • Monitor your weight to increase your motivation
  • Be consistent with your diet plan and exercise routine

Liposuction is also used to remove excess fat in the body
